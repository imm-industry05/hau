Valorant Champions: What happen to APAC

Sept 13 2022

Articles

2 Comments

The Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) is a global competitive esports tournament series for the video game Valorant organised by Riot Games, the game's developers. The series runs multiple events throughout each season, culminating in Valorant Champions, the top-level event of the tour.

Valorant

Coming into the VALORANT Champions 2022, Asia Pacific (APAC) VALORANT has transformed from a once-minor region to a significant powerhouse that is home to championship-caliber candidates. From Team Secret and XERXIA’s outstanding playoff performances at the inaugural Champions to Paper Rex’s first-runner-up finish at this year’s final Masters, APAC has repeatedly risen up to the largest platform in the world and outperformed everyone else. Sadly, everything good must come to an end, and for us APAC supporters, it was heartbreaking to see every one of our APAC delegates leave this year’s Champions. This is how all of our APAC teams were eliminated from the most important tournament of the year, having said that.
