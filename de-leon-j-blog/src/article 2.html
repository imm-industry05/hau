Kaguya Sama Season 3 takes top 1 spot

Sept 13 2022

Articles

2 Comments

Kaguya-sama: Love Is War -Ultra Romantic- has recently become the number one rated anime on MyAnimeList, dethroning the 2009 classic, Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood. The third season of the hit romantic comedy series currently has a score of 9.17, compared to Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood's (FMAB) 9.14 score.

Kaguya sama

Kaguya-sama: Love Is War -Ultra Romantic- just overtook the 2009 cult favorite Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood as the highest rated anime on MyAnimeList. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood (FMAB) presently has a score of 9.14, while the third season of the popular romantic comedy series gets a score of 9.17. However, it’s important to note that FMAB has received ratings from 1.8 million people, compared to only 188,000 for Kaguya-Sama. This isn’t the first FMAB has slipped from the number one spot either, as other anime series such as Fruits Basket, Gintama and even movies like Your Name have briefly held that spot as well. After more people rank Kaguya-sama: Love Is War -Ultra Romantic- on MyAnimeList, FMAB will probably retake its reign. Despite this, Kaguya-Sama: Love Is War has still accomplished a remarkable feat because there aren’t many rom-com anime series that could pull off the same ratings. Fans of Kaguya-sama: Love Is War -Ultra Romantic- continue to adore the show since the third season is every bit as amusing as the first two. The primary protagonists of the program, Kaguya and Shirogane, also saw significant development throughout the third season.
