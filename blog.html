Candace

Candace is the guardian of Aaru Village, maintaining peace from those who would threaten it. She is a kind woman, ensuring that all visitors to the village are treated fairly and does not care about their affiliation as long as they respect the peace, which she finds rewarding enough for her duties. As such, she does not forgive those who threaten the village's safety.
